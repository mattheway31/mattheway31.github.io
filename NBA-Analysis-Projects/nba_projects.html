Positional 3PT Shooting Trends (2024-2025 Season)

With the rise of 3-Point Shooting in the modern-day NBA, finding which positions shoot the 3-ball most efficiently league-wide gives me a great starting point.

View Bar Graph (PDF)

The Shooting Guard position was both the highest volume 3-point shooting position and the most effiecient at it during the 2024-2025 season. Historically, the Power Forward and Center positions are considered the "non-traditional" shooters and we can see that they are still not comparable with the other positions in regards to volume and they are still the least efficient shooters in the league.

Positional 3PT Shooting Trends By Team (2024-2025 Season)

Analyzing each team's 3-point efficiency by position will help us determine which teams in today's era are relying more and more on non-traditional 3-point shooters.

View Heat Map (PDF)

This heatmap compares each team's positional 3P % against the league average (36.3 %). Green indicates high efficiency (above league average) and red indicates low efficiency (below league average).

As you can see, teams like the L.A. Clippers, Denver Nuggets, New York Knicks, Golden State Warriors, and Indiana Pacers are showing heavy reliance on efficient shooting from their big men, indicating modern, spaced-out offensive gameplans. A team like the Clevland Cavaliers shows exceptional 3-point shooting across all five positions, demonstrating a well-balanced offense in terms of 3-point shooting. For the Portland Trailblazers, each position aside from the PF position is shooting the 3-ball below league average.
